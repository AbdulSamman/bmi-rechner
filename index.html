What is BMI?

The body mass index, or BMI for short, is the most common formula for calculating weight calculation. It is calculated from the ratio of the body weight in kilograms and the height in meters squared. Depending on the level of the calculated value, the German Society for Nutrition (DGE) distinguishes five categories for the evaluation: Underweight,Normal weight, Overweight, Extreme overweight(obesity) and massive obesity.
